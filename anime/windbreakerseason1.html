About Wind Breaker

Wind Breaker is a high-energy anime series that follows Haruka Sakura, a strong and rebellious transfer student who joins Furin High School—a school known for its fierce fighters who protect their town. As Haruka navigates intense battles and forms bonds with unlikely allies, the series blends action, heart, and powerful themes of friendship and justice.

A heartfelt thank you to the creators of Wind Breaker for bringing such an inspiring and thrilling story to life. Your work has touched fans around the world with its powerful characters, stunning animation, and emotional storytelling.

We also extend our gratitude to Muse India for making Wind Breaker accessible to fans across the region. Your efforts in promoting and streaming quality anime are deeply appreciated by the entire community.

Thank you for everything!
